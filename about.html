Our Mission

Every purchase helps provide food, shelter, and care for animals in need.

Wear your heart for pets.

Paws & Threads was founded with a simple belief: your clothing should reflect what you care about. For pet lovers, that means wearing your love for animals — and making a tangible difference with every purchase.

We design premium, comfortable apparel that looks great and feels even better. Each piece is thoughtfully crafted with attention to detail, using sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing.

10% of every purchase is donated to animal welfare charities, including local shelters, rescue organizations, and veterinary care programs. We rotate our charity partners quarterly so your purchases support a wide range of animal causes.

We believe in transparency: every quarter, we publish a full accounting of our donations so you can see exactly how your purchases are making an impact.
